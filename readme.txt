* You get paid for clicks on products in your Shopping Guide / banners
* Choose from 2 themes
* Choose colours to suit your site
* Choose a name for your shopping guide
* Choose your favourite categories
* Choose your favourite products

* Français/English available


== Installation ==

= Installation Instructions: = 

1. Create a free account via: https://espaceclients.buzzea.com/blogueur/ (to pay you for clicks you earn) 
2. Choose a shopping guide to install from 'Pages/Guides/Bannières shopping' from the menu at Buzzea.
3. Download and install the plugin into your wp-content/plugins/ directory.
4. Go to 'Buzzea Shopping' in your admin settings menu
5. Enter your password (provided in the Buzzea admin area)
6. That's it! Ready to go. 

= Customization Instructions: = 

1. Now you can choose a theme
2. Enter the name of your Shopping Guide
3. Select favourite categories and products to show
4. Install banners on your site using widgets
5. Install a shopping guide on your site using shortcode [cdp_buzzea] in a post/page
